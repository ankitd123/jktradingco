About Us

Established in 1989, J. K. Steels is a trusted stockist and dealer of all type of Mild Steel (MS), Stainless Steel (SS) & Alloy Steels products. Based in Mumbai, India, we have built a reputation for providing reliable products to our customers.

We cater to clients across India and internationally, offering quality materials at competitive prices with a focus on timely delivery and customer satisfaction.
